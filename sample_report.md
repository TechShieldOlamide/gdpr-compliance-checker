## What This Tool Does NOT Check

This tool performs surface-level automated checks only. It does not replace a full legal compliance audit. It cannot check:

- Accuracy of your privacy policy content
- Whether your actual data practices match your policy
- Backend data handling and security controls
- Data Processing Agreements with third parties
- Staff training and internal compliance procedures
- Cross-border data transfer safeguards
